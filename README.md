# Lecture Notes on Statistical Inference

**Author:** Dr. Francisco Javier Rubio (UCL)  
**Current version:** 1st January 2023  
**Document:** [`StatInf2023.pdf`](StatInf2023.pdf)

## Overview

These lecture notes provide a self-contained introduction to the foundations of
**statistical inference** at undergraduate and postgraduate level. The notes are primarily intended
for students of statistics and related disciplines, and cover both frequentist
and Bayesian perspectives. They closely follow the textbooks cited in the
bibliography, with some additions and clarifications by the author.
